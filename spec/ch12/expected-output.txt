KEY OBSERVATIONS FOR THE READER
================================================================================

1. MESSAGE ENVELOPE — 7 fields, every message, no exceptions
2. REQUEST/RESPONSE — ask-and-wait with correlation_id linking the pair
3. STREAMING — incremental chunks + stream_end for long-running tasks
4. ARTIFACTS — typed work products, not descriptions. Parseable and attributed.
5. CORRELATION IDS — one thread per conversation chain. Pull the ID, get the story.
6. TIMEOUT — unresponsive peers don't block forever. Configurable, reportable.
7. CHAIN TRACING — 10 messages, 3 agents, 3 artifacts, 1 correlation_id.
8. The bridge to Ch 13: agents talk when told to. What if they talked on their own?
