================================================================================
KEY OBSERVATIONS FOR THE READER
================================================================================

1. CHECKPOINTS — save at boundaries, restore on startup, resume from last step
2. CRASH RECOVERY — context_summary gives the LLM enough to continue (not identical, close enough)
3. DISTRIBUTED TRACING — trace context propagates through message envelopes, waterfall shows the story
4. PARENT-CHILD SPANS — tree structure across agents, not just flat list
5. VERSION COMPATIBILITY — three outcomes: compatible, degraded, incompatible
6. MIXED-VERSION — negotiate to lowest common capability, degrade features not function
7. IDEMPOTENCY — check before process, record after complete, duplicates return cached result
8. SAGA + IDEMPOTENCY — retry saga from scratch, completed steps are skipped automatically
9. The bridge to Ch 15: the swarm is durable, observable, and safely deployable — but it's a closed system. External tools and agents are next.
